About Us

The Ashwa Mobility Foundation is a distinguished student-led organization based at the RV College of Engineering in Bengaluru, India. Established in 2003 as Ashwa Racing, the foundation has evolved into a multifaceted entity dedicated to advancing mobility engineering through innovative projects and competitions.

The foundation's mission is to prepare students for industry readiness by conceptualizing, designing, and delivering vehicles of world-class standards. It aims to provide a platform for students to interact with and learn from the industry, exhibit India's engineering capabilities on a global stage, and develop sustainable mobility solutions for the masses. The vision is to empower budding engineers to showcase their talent in national and international events, exposing them to top technical and managerial practices employed in the industry.
